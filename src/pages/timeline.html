Welcome to the

CUBO+ Journey

01
January 2021

President Bukele inaugurates the first CUBO

January 9th 2021

President Nayib Bukele inaugurated the first CUBO in January 2021. CUBOs aim to create better spaces for development and opportunities for communities, especially for young people.

September 2021
02

Bitcoin Law in El Salvador

September 7th 2021

On September 7, 2021, the Bitcoin Law came into effect in El Salvador, making it the first country in the world to adopt Bitcoin as legal tender. This marked a significant technological and economic advancement for the country, positioning it as a center for Bitcoin from that moment forward.

03
January 2023

CUBO+

January 2023

The National Bitcoin Office partnered with Fulgur Ventures to create CUBO+, a program designed to train Bitcoin developers in El Salvador, maintaining President Nayib Bukele's vision for CUBOs.

March 2023
04

CUBO+ Devs Generation Call

March 2023

The call was open for CUBO+ Devs Generation. The call began at Don Bosco University in Soyapango and later expanded to other universities, where many were able to learn about this incredible opportunity.

05
April 2023

CUBO+ Devs 2023 begins

April 23rd 2023

56 students were selected for the preparation phase, online classes, and experts in the field as teachers, including Giacomo Zucco, Alekos Filini, Rikki, Ricardo Masutti, Walter, and Jan Burgers.

May 2023
06

Saifedean Ammous Lecure

May 22nd 2023

A few weeks after starting the preparatory period, the students had an in-person masterclass by Saifedean Ammous, where he spoke about his book "The Bitcoin Standard."

07
June 2023

Bootcamp selection process

May - June 2023

After weeks of classes and careful evaluation, 21 of the top students were selected to attend a 2-week in-person Bootcamp (1 week in San Salvador, 1 week in El Zonte). During this Bootcamp, students were provided with accommodation and meals.

July 2023
08

Bootcamp

July 2nd - July 16th

Los estudiantes seleccionados tuvieron la oportunidad de aprender mucho y convivir con grandes mentores y lideres en la industria de Bitcoin, entre ellos Adam Gibson, Ajelex, Rogzy, Asi0, Gabriel Comte, Peter Todd, Alekos Filini, Daniela Brozzoni.

09

Hackathon and Bootcamp finale

July 12nd - July 16th 2023

To conclude the bootcamp, students put into practice all the knowledge they acquired during the program. Six teams participated in a highly competitive hackathon, with each team proposing a solution. The students had 3 days to work on their projects, after which a panel of experts in the field chose the most innovative project.

10

Jimmy Song Bonus Course

July 18th - July 22nd 2023

After the two intensive weeks, the top 11 students had the opportunity to participate in Jimmy Song's advanced course "Programming Bitcoin". This was undoubtedly an extra resource that the top students could take advantage of, and they later shared this acquired knowledge with their fellow peers.

11
August 2023

Beginning of Internships

August 2023

After the bootcamp, many companies began hiring the new CUBO+ students. The first technical Bitcoin course in El Salvador concluded, creating new opportunities for 21 young individuals and meeting the needs of businesses. The first student hired was Moisés, at the Salvadoran company Tiankii, specializing in Bitcoin.

October 2023
12

Trip to Europe

October 5th 2023

2 months after concluding CUBO+ Devs Generation 2023, the top 6 students, who demonstrated their Proof of Work during and after the course, had the opportunity to visit Tuscany and Lugano.

13

Tuscany Lightning Summit 2023

October 11th - October 13th 2023

The CUBO students had the opportunity to attend the Tuscany Lightning Summit 2023 in person, a two-day event filled with workshops, lectures, conferences, and networking sessions, focused on developers specialized in the Lightning Network.

14

Build the Builders 2023

October 15th - October 18th 2023

After the Lightning Summit, the students attended the Build the Builders 2023 conference, which focused on the development of the Bitcoin network.

15

Lugano PlanB Forum 2023

October 20th - October 21st 2023

To conclude their training, the top students from CUBO had the opportunity to attend the PlanB Forum in Lugano, Switzerland. At this forum, the new PlanB Network initiative was presented.

16

Return to El Salvador

October 2023

After nearly 3 weeks, the students of CUBO+ returned home, undoubtedly strengthened after a trip filled with learning, opportunities, and networking.

17
November 2023

Adopting Bitcoin

November 7th - November 9th

Four months after completing the bootcamp, the CUBO+ students returned to participate in the "Legends of Lightnings" hackathon. After 3 days of intense development, a team from CUBO+ claimed the first place, showcasing why CUBO+ is synonymous with excellence in teaching Bitcoin at a technical level.

January 2024
18

First Meetup

January 19th 2024

At the beginning of 2024, CUBO+ decided to organize its first meetup, where some graduates and new prospective students attended to engage in conversations and enjoy a fruitful time of knowledge and learning.

19
March 2024

CUBO+ website

March 2024

Nearly a year after the inception of the idea for CUBO+, CUBO+ launched its first website created by its own graduated students. Today, this website greatly helps more Salvadorans learn about the opportunities offered by CUBO+.

20

Applications Open for CUBO+ ECON Generation

March 2024

CUBO+ has opened its applications for the new generation, this time focusing on university students from all fields. CUBO+ ECON Generation aims to include both technical and non-technical individuals, meaning programming knowledge is not a requirement to participate and learn.

21
April 2024

Luke Dashjr Lecture

April 2nd 2024

Before starting CUBO+ ECON Generation, students had the opportunity to attend a lecture by Luke Dashjr, one of the early developers of Bitcoin Core. During this lecture, students asked Luke questions about the Bitcoin network and expressed excitement for the beginning of ECON Generation.

22

CUBO+ Econ 2024 Begins

April 2024

After 3 weeks of applications, 81 students were selected for CUBO+ Econ Generation. They began receiving in-person classes, starting with 3 introductory masterclasses by Alejandro Muñoz from Accelerate, Ajelex and Ernesto Quezada from PlanB Network, and Darvin Otero from Tiankii.

23
May 2024

PlanB Biz School

May 2024

The 81 students were awarded scholarships to attend classes at PlanB Biz School. The course typically costs $2000 USD for international students, but Salvadoran students from CUBO+ Econ Generation received a full scholarship, granting them access to the course at no cost.

24

Pierre Rochard Lecture

May 4th 2023

CUBO+ had the opportunity to host the guest star lecture by Pierre Rochard from Riot Platforms. Students had the chance to learn about Mining Technology and Economics.

25

David Puell Lecture

May 22nd 2024

The next guest star lecture was delivered by David Puell, Research Associate at ARK Investment Management. He provided an insightful presentation based on ARK's Annual Research Report to the students of CUBO+.

26

Yorick De Mombynes

May 25th 2024

The third guest star lecture was delivered by Yorick de Mombynes, who has political experience in France. Yorick gave a masterclass to the students of CUBO+, focusing on Bitcoin as Open Money and Open Society principles.

27

Jack Mallers Lecture

May 31st 2024

La cuarta conferencia Guest Star fue presentada por el reconocido Jack Mallers, CEO de Strike, una billetera ampliamente utilizada a nivel global para Bitcoin. Jack habló sobre "Building on Bitcoin", explicando a los estudiantes cómo ha evolucionado el dinero desde tiempos pasados hasta hoy en día y cómo Bitcoin representa el futuro.

June 2024
28

Yan Pritzker

June 7th 2024

The fifth Guest Star Lecture was given by Yan Pritzker, CTO of Swan Bitcoin. Yan delivered a highly informative session for CUBO+ Econ Generation students, discussing his book "Inventing Bitcoin". He covered topics ranging from the Gold Standard, inflation, the decline in the dollar's purchasing power, to how Bitcoin operates today.

29

Case Studies Assignments

June 15th 2024

To demonstrate their acquired knowledge, all students had to work in groups on an assignment for two weeks, creating a case study provided by Salvadoran companies. Following this, each group presented their case study live and were evaluated by the same companies.

30

LARP Session

June 15th 2024

After the students' presentations on their case studies, a Live Action Role-Play (LARP) session was organized. In this session, two graduate students from CUBO+ who had traveled to Mexico to learn from Lisa Neigut at Base58 shared their knowledge with the new generation of students. The game involved acting as nodes and simulating the operation of the Bitcoin network.

31

Eric Yakes

June 20th 2024

The latest lecture at the CUBO+ node was given by Eric Yakes. Eric spoke about his book The 7th Property, discussed Bitcoin, and the monetary revolution. It was undoubtedly a masterful class. .

32

Students Selected

June 2024

Three of the standout students during their case study presentation were selected to attend the PlanB Summer School in Lugano, Switzerland. Jorge, Maureen, and Isis had the opportunity to travel, learn, network, and of course, experience new things.

33
July 2024

PlanB Summer School

July 1st - July 6th 2024

The three scholarship students from CUBO+ attended PlanB Summer School, an exclusive school for the top 21 students. During the school, the students met new classmates, had the opportunity to chat with their mentors, and, of course, enjoyed a week full of extensive learning.

32

School Conclution and graduation

July 6th

The 3 CUBO+ students, along with the 18 international students, graduated in a ceremony held at the Lugano City Hall. The ceremony was attended by the Ambassador of El Salvador to Switzerland Yessenia Lozano, the Director of PlanB Network Giacomo Zucco, and a representative of the Mayor of Lugano. During the ceremony, the students received their participation diplomas.
